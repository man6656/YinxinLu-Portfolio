Clock Tower

This tower was inspired by the clock tower at my bachelor stage university, with the crown serving as the main design element. The use of gold on the crown and clock protection ring creates a sense of nobility and elegance, while the plaster white on the main body of the building helps to clearly define its boundaries and conveys a feeling of purity.

Timidity

This is an experimental work in which I try to explain introversion and depression through documentary interviews and discussions. In order to show the thoughts and struggles of people with depression, and to give the audience some knowledge about it, I have made some creative visuals in the beginning and the end of the film to avoid the problem of lengthy and preachy documentary dialogues.
